[Super Shape Shift](https://clementinefruity.itch.io/bit-adventure) is a platformer with a minimalist outline aesthetic. Switch between shapes with different abilities to solve each level and beat your best time and get the bonus collectables.

Made in one week for Weekly Game Jam #140
